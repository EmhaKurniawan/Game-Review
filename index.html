Death Stranding

Death Stranding is a video game with a genre of walking simulator taking the setting of post-apocalyptic in United States of America. It was released in 2019 by Kojima Production. A new game mechanic called social-strand system is introduced here. It means gamers can stay connected though they are not in the same server like MMORPG. It is worth playing.

Ghost of Tsushima

Ghost of Tsushima is a video game with a genre of traditional-weaponry-third-person fighter. it takes the setting of Japan in 1274 when Mongol invaded Japan through Tsushima island. This video game offer unique game mechanic because it has samurai stances which are beneficial for taking particular enemy weapons. It also offer two playstyles: heroic or stealth. The story and the plot is really satisfying.
